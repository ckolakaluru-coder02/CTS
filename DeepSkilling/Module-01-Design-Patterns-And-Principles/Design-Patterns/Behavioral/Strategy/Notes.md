\# Strategy Pattern



The Strategy Pattern is a behavioral design pattern that allows selecting an algorithm or behavior at runtime.



It defines a family of algorithms and makes them interchangeable.



\## Real-time Example



Payment methods like Credit Card, UPI, and PayPal can be implemented using Strategy Pattern.



\## Advantages



\- Allows changing behavior at runtime

\- Avoids long if-else conditions

\- Improves flexibility

\- Follows Open/Closed Principle
